NammaFlow System User Guide

Data-driven Congestion Mitigation

NammaFlow is an advanced event-driven traffic coordination center for Bengaluru. The platform ingests real-time spatial, temporal, and situational traffic incident vectors to prescribe tactical dispatch plans.

8,100+ Historical Incidents
99.1% Classification Acc
50+ Police Jurisdictions

To begin, explore the dashboard tabs to visualize traffic patterns, simulate new incidents on the planner tab, dispatch responses, and submit ground feedback to dynamically calibrate the ML models.

Traffic Simulator & Blueprints

The simulator allows you to forecast the congestion duration and severity of planned or unplanned road events:

  • Place Snaps: Click anywhere on the map on the Planner tab to snap coordinates, or drag the markers. The red marker is the incident source, the green marker is the reopening/recovery endpoint.
  • Run Analysis: Fill out the event details (e.g. event cause, peak commute hours, segment closure requirements) and click **Run Impact Analysis** to trigger the Random Forest classifier and regressor models.
  • Actionable Dispatch: Check off the dispatch phases to deploy manpower, place warning barricades upstream, and activate routing detours.

Closed-Loop Feedback & Retraining

The post-event learning loop keeps ML model predictions aligned with actual ground operations:

  • Ground Feedback: Submit the actual duration, officers deployed, barricades used, and rate the effectiveness of the diversion blueprint.
  • Augment Logs: Submitted data points are appended to the feedback.json ground ledger in real-time.
  • Retrain Model: Click **Retrain Model** inside the Performance Curve card to trigger scikit-learn model retraining. This fits encoders and refits RandomForest classifiers/regressors on the updated feedback dataset.
