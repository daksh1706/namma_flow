How AstraFlow Learns

1
Predict & Recommend

Random Forest models forecast congestion parameters and recommend manpower, barricades, and diversions based on 8,000+ historical incidents.

2
Ground Officer Override

Ground officers deploy resources and log exact adjustments, corrections, and results inside the app.

3
Feedback Assimilation

The system stores feedback, calibrates predictions, and logs discrepancies. Over time, models are retrained using this ground-truth data, closing the loop.
